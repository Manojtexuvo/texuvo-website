About Us

India's Solid Waste Sourcing & Recycling Ecosystem

Texuvo Recycling Solution Pvt. Ltd. is committed to building a sustainable ecosystem for recyclable materials in India. We work at the intersection of waste generation and responsible recovery — channeling industrial waste, post-consumer materials, and manufacturing scrap into productive circular supply chains.

We partner with industries, traders, waste generators, recyclers and manufacturers to ensure efficient material sourcing, resource recovery and responsible solid waste management practices across India.

Recyclable Materials

Materials We Deal In

From industrial textile waste to high-value metal scrap — we source, connect and recover across all major solid waste and recyclable material categories.

Textile Waste

Industrial cutting waste, fabric offcuts, garment scraps, and post-industrial textile waste from manufacturing units.

Fabric · Cutting Waste · Industrial

Paper Waste

Old corrugated cartons (OCC), white ledger paper, newsprint, mixed paper, and industrial paper scrap for repulping.

OCC · Newsprint · Office Waste

Plastic Waste

Post-consumer and post-industrial HDPE, PET, PP, PVC, and mixed plastic waste supporting circular economy solutions.

HDPE · PET · PP · PVC

Aluminum Scrap

Aluminium extrusion, sheet scrap, UBC (used beverage cans), casting scrap, and industrial aluminum offcuts.

Extrusion · UBC · Casting

Copper Scrap

High-value copper wire, tubing, bus bars, motor windings, and manufacturing offcuts for recycling and secondary smelting.

Wire · Tubes · Bus Bar

Iron & Metal Scrap

HMS 1&2, shredded steel, stainless steel scrap, cast iron, and mixed ferrous/non-ferrous industrial metal scrap.

HMS · Shredded · Stainless
