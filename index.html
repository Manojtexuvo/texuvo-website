Our Story

Texuvo was founded with a vision to create a more sustainable textile industry. By connecting waste generators, recyclers and manufacturers, we help reduce landfill waste, promote resource recovery and support the transition toward a circular textile economy.

Who We Are

Texuvo Recycling Solution Pvt. Ltd. focuses on textile waste sourcing, trading and recycling support services across India.

Our objective is to build an efficient supply chain that creates value from textile waste while supporting recyclers, manufacturers and sustainability initiatives.
