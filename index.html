What We Handle

Materials We Deal In

From industrial textile waste to high-value metal scrap — we source, connect and recover across all major recyclable material categories.

Textile Waste

Industrial textile waste, cutting waste, and recyclable fabric materials sourced from garment manufacturers.

Paper Waste

Paper scrap and recyclable paper materials sourced for efficient resource recovery and repulping.

Plastic Waste

Recyclable plastic materials supporting circular economy — post-consumer and post-industrial grades.

Aluminum Scrap

Recyclable aluminum materials supporting sustainable metal recovery and secondary aluminium production.

Copper Scrap

High-value copper scrap for recycling and manufacturing — wires, tubes, and industrial offcuts.

Iron & Metal Scrap

Industrial metal scrap of all grades sourced for efficient recycling, reuse, and downstream manufacturing.
